Lettuce

Lactuca sativa

Lettuce is one of the most common vegetables. It is very easy to grow, and does not require lots of maintenance.

The plant thrives in temperatures between 60 to 65 degrees Fahrenheit. Temperatures must remain above freezing at all times in order for the plant to stay alive.

Lettuce needs to be watered when the soil is dry. Once or twice a week is sufficient in watering. The plant grows best in cooler climates, but can also grow very well in warmer climates as long as shade is present.
